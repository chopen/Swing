# 2026 NCAA Tournament First Round Predictions — MVIX + MRVI

## Methodology

Predictions generated using The Swing's momentum volatility metrics:
- **MRVI** (Rolling 5-game Momentum Relative Volatility Index) — higher = volatility trending favorably. Best CBB single predictor at 61.1% accuracy over 270 qualifying games.
- **MVIX** (Rolling Momentum Volatility Index) — lower = calmer/more controlled play.
- MRVI weighted 2x vs MVIX 1x for CBB per our 1,220-game analysis.
- When signals conflict, MRVI takes priority (strongest CBB predictor).

## Important Caveats

MVIX/MRVI measures **momentum texture, not team quality**. Small-conference teams can have favorable MRVI profiles because they dominate weaker opponents. The raw model predicts 14 upsets including two 16-over-1 seeds — unrealistic. Predictions below are tempered with seeding context. MVIX/MRVI should be combined with team quality metrics (e.g., KenPom, NET rankings) for production-grade predictions.

## Methodology Notes

- Rolling MVIX computed from last 10 games (or fewer if insufficient history)
- Rolling MRVI computed from last 5 games with MRVI data available
- MRVI weighted 2x over MVIX based on 1,220-game CBB validation (MRVI: 54.3% single-game, 61.1% rolling 5-game)
- Seed used as tiebreaker and override for extreme mismatches (16-over-1, 15-over-2)
- Predictions do not account for injuries, venue, travel, matchup specifics, or team quality ratings
- Based on momentum texture analysis only — should be combined with traditional metrics for comprehensive predictions
