All MVIX* and MRVI* values are **conference-adjusted**. Power conf (B10/B12/SEC/ACC/BE) = 1.0x. Strong mid (WCC/MWC/MVC) = 0.85x. AAC/A10 = 0.80x. Mid-major = 0.70x. Low-major = 0.60-0.65x.

**Top seed protection (R1-E8):** 2.5x for 1/2 seeds. Removed from Final Four.
**Close-metrics rule (R1-E8):** Combined adj diff < 8 = 2x seed bonus.

MVIX* and MRVI* columns show conference-adjusted rolling averages (10-game MVIX, 5-game MRVI).
