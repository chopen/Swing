| Signal | Weight | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **MRVI** (Rolling 5-game) | 2x | Direction of volatility — higher = trending favorably (61.1% CBB accuracy) |
| **MVIX** (Rolling 10-game) | 1x | Magnitude of volatility — lower = calmer/more controlled |

**Close-metrics rule:** When the combined MVIX + MRVI difference between two teams is less than 8, the higher-seeded team receives a 2x weight bonus. This prevents the model from picking upsets based on negligible metric differences.
